About

Ground.Vu was founded by Myles Sutherland 2015 after a decade in geospatial at both Trimble and ESRI.

Our mission is to enable better GIS through deploying the latest technologies in data collection.

In our short existance, we have captured from drone, manned aircraft, cars, trains and on foot. We're looking forward to you challenging us to do high quality data capture in new and difficult circumstances.
